JOMPO Cassava Innovation Lab

Our Cassava Innovation Lab transforms cassava leaves, stems, and tubers into high-value products for food security, nutrition, and income generation.

Cassava Leaves & Stems – Nutrition & Agribusiness

Leaves are used for vegetable dishes, juice, and livestock feed. Stems support propagation, mulching, and regenerative farming practices.

Cassava Leaf Herbal Soap – Commercial Products

Herbal soap made from cassava leaves, olive oil, palm oil, and NaOH. Safe pH confirmed; packaged for sale, generating revenue and consumer impact.

Cassava Tubers – Food Products (Chips, Flour, Bread, Cakes, Chapatis)

Tubers are processed into chips, flour, bread, cakes, and chapatis, supporting rural incomes and urban market supply.
