Cassava Value-Chain Prototype

The cassava prototype focuses on transforming cassava leaves, stems, and tubers into high-value products that support food security, nutrition, and agribusiness development.

✔ Benefits of Cassava Leaves

Cassava leaves are rich in protein, vitamins A & C, and minerals. They can be processed into livestock feed, human food (vegetable), micronutrient supplements, and juice blends.

✔ Benefits of Cassava Stems

Cassava stems are essential for propagation and can be used for: planting materials, mulching, briquettes, and regenerative agriculture.

✔ Benefits of Cassava Tubers

Cassava tubers are used for food processing, flour production, starch extraction, and bioenergy. They serve as a major food and income source for rural communities.
