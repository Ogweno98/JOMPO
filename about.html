Who We Are

JOMPO Digital Solutions is a visionary organization at the intersection of agriculture, digital innovation, and environmental conservation. Our mission is to empower rural communities and farmers across Kenya through digital transformation, green technology, and inclusive opportunities.

What We Do

  • Deploy renewable energy solutions including solar panels and borehole drilling
  • Support smart farming with IoT devices and precision agriculture tools
  • Deliver practical training and mentorship for agricultural entrepreneurs
  • Promote sustainable land use, tree planting, and climate adaptation
  • Empower youth through Dream Soccer and digital literacy initiatives
